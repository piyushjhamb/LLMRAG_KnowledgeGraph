Objective:
Create a structured Question & Answer format analysis based on the attached win/loss statement. The goal is to extract key insights regarding the decision-making process, vendor evaluation, selection criteria, and areas for improvement.

Key Q&A Categories:
1. Deal Summary
Q: What was the outcome of this deal?
Q: What was the deal size, and which industry does the company belong to?
Q: Who was the decision-maker, and what was their role?
Q: When was the decision finalized?
2. Competitive Analysis
Q: Which vendors were evaluated?
Q: Who was the strongest competitor?
Q: Who was the incumbent vendor, and why were they displaced?
Q: What were the primary reasons for selecting UKG over the competitors?
3. Key Decision Criteria
Q: What were the most important factors in the selection process?
Q: How did UKG perform against competitors in these key criteria?
Q: Were there any major gaps between UKG and the strongest competitor?
4. Strengths & Winning Factors
Q: What aspects of UKG’s solution stood out the most?
Q: How did UKG's reputation and experience impact the decision?

5. Areas of Improvement
Q: Were there any concerns about UKG that could impact future deals?

Q: What could UKG have done differently to improve its chances?
6. Competitor Weaknesses & Loss Factors
Q: Why did the competitors lose the deal?
Q: Were there any concerns about Paylocity and Rippling that contributed to their loss?
Q: Did pricing, product functionality, or customer service impact the competitors' performance?
7. Future Considerations
Q: Will the company consider UKG for future business?
Q: What factors could influence their decision in future evaluations?
Q: Are there any upcoming opportunities where UKG could position itself better?
Deliverables:

Provide a structured Q&A document with detailed responses for each section.
